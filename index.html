OUR STORY

Dogshop was founded by Kritsakorn and Jason , two animal-loving friends setting out to change the world for the sake of humankind's best friend and our Planet. Our friendship began in Zagreb, Croatia, in 2013. After spending five years Oceans apart, Anka in Europe and Christina in Canada, we reunited in Germany in 2018. Through a series of divine interventions, we began our quest towards resurrecting the human connection to dogs. We are passionate about changing the world by preaching essential lessons we can learn from dogs and bringing social awareness to our intrinsic connection to dogs. Three things that powerfully drove us forward were heart, will, and passion.
